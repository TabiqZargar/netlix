Top Gun: Maverick

8.2/10 | Hollywod | English

Top Gun: Maverick (2022) follows Pete “Maverick” Mitchell as he returns to the elite Navy fighter weapons school to train a new generation of pilots for a near-impossible mission. Still pushing the limits as a test pilot, Maverick must confront the ghosts of his past, the legacy of Goose, and his uneasy relationship with Goose’s son, Rooster. The film blends high-octane aerial combat with themes of mentorship, sacrifice, and earning the right to fly on one’s own terms.
